## Why?

Claude Code's built-in `MEMORY.md` is great for short personal preferences, but it doesn't scale to dozens of apps and hundreds of standards. A real notes backend does — and most people already have one (Trilium, Obsidian, Notion).

This plugin closes the gap. Claude reads your knowledge base before acting, writes back when something noteworthy happens, and the discipline rules stop it filling the brain with noise.
